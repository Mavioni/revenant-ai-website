Engram

Warming up.

Engram runs as its own deployment. This route embeds it inside Yunis AI, but the iframe source isn't configured yet.

To wire it up: add VITE_ENGRAM_URL=https://mavioni.github.io/Project-Engram/ to .env.local in the site repo, restart the dev server, and this iframe will load the live Engram build. Point it at a custom domain (like engram.revenant.ai) once you have one.
